What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a standardized metric that represents your overall academic performance across all completed semesters. It provides a comprehensive view of your educational journey and consistency in learning.

This advanced calculation considers all subjects, credit hours, and grading systems to give you an accurate representation of your academic excellence that institutions and employers worldwide recognize and trust.

Why CGPA Matters

Your CGPA serves as a crucial benchmark for university admissions, scholarship eligibility, internship opportunities, and job placements. It demonstrates your intellectual capabilities, work ethic, and commitment to academic excellence.

Many prestigious institutions use CGPA as a primary screening criterion, making it essential for students to maintain competitive averages. A strong CGPA opens doors to graduate programs, research opportunities, and career advancement.
