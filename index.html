University-Specific Conversion Formulas 🎓

CBSE (India)

Used by most Indian schools and universities

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

Mumbai University

Specific formula for Mumbai University students

Percentage = (CGPA × 7.1) + 12

VTU (Visvesvaraya Technological University)

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Is the CGPA to percentage conversion the same for all universities?

No, different universities use different conversion formulas. While many follow the standard formulas (like CGPA × 9.5 for 10-point scale), some have their own specific methods. Always check with your university's official guidelines.

Can I convert percentage back to CGPA?

Yes! For the 10-point scale: CGPA = Percentage ÷ 9.5. For other scales, reverse the formulas: CGPA = (Percentage × Max CGPA) ÷ 100. For example, for a 4.0 scale: CGPA = (Percentage × 4) ÷ 100.

What if my university uses a different grading scale?

If your scale isn't listed (like 7.0 or 12.0), you can still use our calculator. First convert your CGPA to a standard scale (like 4.0 or 10.0) using your university's guidelines, then apply the standard conversion. Alternatively, contact the receiving institution for their preferred method.

How accurate is this calculator?

Our calculator uses widely accepted formulas from major educational boards. However, for official purposes, always verify with your specific institution as some universities have unique conversion methods. We recommend using our result as a reference point.
