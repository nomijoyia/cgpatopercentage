📊 From CGPA to Percentage: The Quick Conversion

Most Pakistani universities use this formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5.
It’s a rough estimate—not an exact science.
Always check your institution’s official conversion policy first.
Some use 9.0 or even 10.0 multipliers.
Clarity prevents last-minute panic during job applications. ✨
